    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Do you think you can work in pitching sales and convincing people to do (buy) stuff? hen the sales career path might be a match made in heaven for you. It involves (as you guessed) making sales to potential customers.

    It also involves effective communication, analytics, and the most important of all, patience. An inquisitive person today will be a customer tomorrow. A sale is a transaction in which the buyer obtains tangible or intangible products, services, or assets in return for money from two or more parties. In the financial markets, a sale can also refer to a pricing agreement between a buyer and a seller for security. You must have good communication skills to be a successful salesperson. A successful salesperson achieves sales goals while remaining courteous and helpful to consumers.

    Description

    A salesperson is responsible for selling products or services that meet customer needs. They identify prospective customers, establish trust, nurture prospects through the company's sales funnel, provide information on offerings, and bring in new customers by convincing people to make a purchase. Choosing a career in sales depends on your experience, company, and industry. It’s a background with a plethora of opportunities if you’re decent at what you do. Customers are greeted, assisted in finding things in the store, and transactions are rung up by the salesperson.

    Skills You Need

    • Negotiation Skills: As a salesperson, you'll have to work out budgets, timetables, and expectations with clients. You'll also need to collaborate with a group of people who share your objectives.
    • Creativity Skills: This is what gives you brownie points in this sector; if you want to stand out in the market, you must be able to think outside the box.
    • Communication Skills: They are a must in this job because you will be dealing with a variety of clientele.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Science or Commerce or Humanities.
    • Graduation: BCom. / BBA / BBM / Diploma in Sales
    • Post Graduation: MBA in Marketing and Sales
