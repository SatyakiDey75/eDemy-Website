    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Do you want to work in a creative field and learn all about internet presence? The, digital marketing is the choice for you!Digital Marketing is the new age market. Right from logos to giant banner advertisements on screen, digital marketing is now a huge requirement for anyone coming into the field of marketing.

    Right down from content writing to scripting advertisements, a lot of attention goes into the attention span of putting across a message digitally. As a digital marketer, the job is to deliver the message as quickly and as impactful as possible. At a high level, digital marketing refers to advertising delivered through digital channels such as search engines, websites, social media, email, and mobile apps.

    Description

    A career in digital marketing can be kick-started right from the comfort of your home. A blogger can successfully captivate minds if he/she can send the right idea across. Selling or buying isn’t the only end goal in digital marketing. The agenda includes adding more traffic to a website (like what’s happening here), forming the image of an organization or simply just conveying a message to the audience. They plan and execute all web, SEO/SEM, database marketing, email, social media, and display advertising campaigns. They also design, build, and maintain our social media presence.

    Skills You Need

    • Management Skills: Working for a large mass requires anticipating future trends and executing plans based on that data.
    • Creativity Skills: It is an important aspect in digital marketing. Bloggers, influencers, creative heads and writers are always successful on creative and original ideas.
    • Communication Skills: It is a strong name in the game. A healthy competition is always useful to get rid of the writer’s block or overcome a sense of stagnation.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Science or Commerce or Humanities.
    • Graduation: Digital Marketing Course / BSc. in Computer Science
    • Post Graduation: MSc. in Computer Science / MA. In English.
