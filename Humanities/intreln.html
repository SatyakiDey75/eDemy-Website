    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Do you want to interact with international state representatives? Then you should pursue international relations. The study of the interactions of nation-states and non-governmental organizations in subjects such as politics, economics, and security is known as international relations. The evolving nature of state and non-state players involved in international decision-making. International relations (sometimes known as international affairs) serves a broad purpose in modern society by attempting to comprehend the causes of conflict and how to keep the peace, within the global system, the nature and exercise of power, the evolving nature of state and non-state actors involved in international decision-making. The role entails discussing, negotiating and mediating with the local government issues about peace and war, trade, commerce, economics, as well as social and cultural aspects.
