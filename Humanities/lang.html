    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Do you want to study languages, and learn about words and their origins? Then you should choose languages as a major! A language is a systematic system of communication used by humans that is based on speech and gestures, sign language, and, in some cases, writing. The grammar of a language is its structure, whereas the vocabulary is its free component. All of us can communicate with each other only because of the medium of language that exists between us. Language as we all know helps us communicate effectively and also understand a lot more about cultures and communities. Linguistics is the scientific study of language. Critical studies of languages, such as language philosophy, the links between language and cognition, and how words express reality, have been argued at least since the time of Gorgias and Plato in ancient Greece.

    Description

    Linguists research language and the elements that influence how people communicate. They can work in a variety of fields, including translation, analysis, research, and interpretation. Linguists work in a variety of settings, including education, government, and other public and commercial organizations. Studying languages and especially foreign languages takes a different kind of dedication. These people would mostly have to interact with a lot of people from various backgrounds. Especially with foreign languages, it could involve travelling also. They would also have to read and be good at analyzing.
