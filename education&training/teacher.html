    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Do you love to share your knowledge and help others improve? If so, then a career in Teaching is just for you. Teachers are responsible for imparting knowledge and educating younger generations of people. They are responsible for the mental development of students at one of their most crucial stages of life and can have a lifelong impact on their students.

    Teachers are, without a doubt, the most important people in our society. They provide children with a sense of purpose, prepare them for success as global citizens, and instil in them a desire to do well and thrive in life. A teacher’s primary responsibility is to impart knowledge to their students, making the ability to effectively communicate very crucial.

    Description

    Working as a teacher can be a very rewarding job. They are responsible for assessing their students, taking into account their strengths and weaknesses and creating ways to impart education on their basis. Beyond simply teaching, they are also responsible for encouraging positive behaviours and discourage misbehaviour. A teacher thus gets the opportunity to help in the overall betterment of their pupils. They must design successful lessons, grade student classwork and provide them feedback, manage classroom materials, navigate the curriculum effectively, and communicate with other staff to accomplish this.

    Skills You Need

    • Communication Skills: A teacher’s primary responsibility is to impart knowledge to their students, making the ability to effectively communicate very crucial.
    • Compassion Skills: One needs to be very empathetic to the needs of their students and show care for them.
    • Creativity Skills: One may need to devise creative ways to impart education to increase the amount of what the students understand and retain.
    • Organization Skills: As one is responsible for the creation of various modules, they need to show organisational ability.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Science or Commerce or Humanities.
    • Graduation: Bachelor course in any field / Bachelor of Elementary Education.
    • Post Graduation: Master of Education.
